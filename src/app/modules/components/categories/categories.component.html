APPLY FOR AN AWARD 10th June - 30th September

We are really excited to be extending our Stop Slavery Awards to recognise the wide range of actors dedicated to ending the scandal of modern slavery and human trafficking. Our new awards will recognise innovators in this space, impactful collaborations between sectors, individuals and CSOs on the frontline of this fight, journalists who raise awareness of this under reported issue as well as Small and medium businesses who are going above and beyond to root out forced labour from their supply chains.
